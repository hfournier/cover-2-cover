Cover2Cover is a 5-piece classic rock dance band created in 2010 by Kelowna luthier and sought after guitarist Jim Rhindress. Jim wanted to bring together the very best classic rock musicians in Kelowna. He wanted players who love to play dance music and who are at the top of their game musically. Most importantly, he wanted musicians who are well... just really great people!

Cover2Cover is composed of four (and sometimes five) committed career musicians in Kelowna, BC: Jim on guitar and vocals, his wife Kim Rhindress on keys and vocals, Robert Vallee on drums and vocals, Cyril Schermann on bass and Mike Mueller on guitar and vocals.

Cover2Cover and has proven itself to be THE classic rock cover band of choice for events large and small ranging from private parties to large corporate events and fundraiser events like the Golden Plate Canadian Culinary Championships (three years in a row) the Earth, Wind & Fire event for Nature Trust of BC (three years in a row) and 11 years for Parks Alive Kelowna.

You might have attended one of the many fundraising events Cover2Cover has provided live music for at Ribfest, Rotary Centre, Mission Hall, The Capri Hotel, The Delta Grand or Parkinson Rec Centre. This is the dance band of choice for discerning party planners and fundraising coordinators!
