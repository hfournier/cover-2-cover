About Cover2Cover

Live music makes every event better! Cover2Cover - Kelowna's classic rock dance band was formed in 2010 and is the brain child of guitarist Jim Rhindress who wanted to create a rock band with the very best players Kelowna has to offer. He wanted a fun classic rock dance band that would not only have the highest level of musicianship but to create a project with great friends who truly enjoy each other's company. You can really feel the energy when these good friends get together to make great music! They take turns fronting the songs, harmonizing and have a ton of fun doing it!

Cover2Cover specializes in songs from the 60s, 70s, 80s and 90s through today with new material being added all the time. We can also perform as a 3-piece or duo depending on the needs of the event.

Our sound system is state of the art and we can provide full stage lighting if needed. We are available for fundraisers, corporate events, private parties and weddings.

Cover2Cover has provided classic rock dance music for such large and prestigious events as the Canadian Culinary Championships three years in a row since 2012, sharing the stage with Canadian rock royalty Barney Bentall, Ed Robertson of the Bare Naked Ladies and Allen Doyle of Great Big Sea, Westjet WineFest, Ribfest and Earth Wind & Fire.

You may also have seen us performing classic rock and dance sets for Festivals Kelowna, Westside Daze, private parties and corporate events.

Related to Cover2Cover

Some of our band members are involved in other projects, which you may be interested in as well.

JR Guitar

JR Guitar is the instrument repair shop of choice for many professional musicians in Kelowna, as well as other guitar stores, pawn shops and music schools in town.

Smitten!

Smitten! is an acoustic and dance music duo I have formed with my wife, Kim Foreman-Rhindress.

We sing a wide variety of acoustic and rock songs. Our repertoire is perfect for any weddings, wineries, corporate functions, or private parties. I play electric guitar, Kim plays keys, and we use backing tracks for the bass and drums. We guarantee you a great night of dance music, classic rock, and 80s & 90s pop songs.

For special occasions like birthdays, anniversaries, or weddings, we can write customs songs. And, we can even deliver a singing telegram.
